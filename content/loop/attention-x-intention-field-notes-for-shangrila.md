# Attention x Intention — Field Notes for Shangri-La

> **Raw note**
> I'm on a week-long "find myself" trip, landing at Shangri-La on Thursday. I'm drawn to the theme of **attention** and how it relates to **intention**. Do they go hand-in-hand? How does this connect to sovereignty, presence, and the way I want to show up with old friends?

---

## 1) Working definitions (short, usable)
- **Attention** = *where the spotlight goes.* Moment-to-moment allocation of awareness.
- **Intention** = *where the arrow points.* The "why/for-what" that orients choices.
- **Sovereignty** = *self-governance of both.* I choose the spotlight **and** the arrow (instead of algorithms, habits, or social pull choosing for me).

**Key relation:** Intention biases what the spotlight seeks; attention refines or corrects the arrow in real time.
**Mantra:** *Aim with intention → steer with attention.*

---

## 2) A simple 2x2 (diagnostic)
| | **Low Intention** | **High Intention** |
|---|---|---|
| **Low Attention** | *Aimless & Scattered* — doomscrolling, social drift. | *Aligned Idea, Scattered Presence* — good "why," but pulled by noise. |
| **High Attention** | *Focused but Unmoored* — hyperfocus on the wrong thing. | **Sweet Spot:** *Focused & Aligned* — presence serving purpose. |

**Use:** At any moment, name your quadrant → take the next nudge toward **Focused & Aligned**.

---

## 3) Festival micro-protocols (carry in your pocket)
**A. The 10-second reset (ABC):**
1) **A**ttention — notice what your eyes/mind are feeding on.
2) **B**reath — one slow 5–6 second inhale + 5–6 second exhale.
3) **C**hoice — ask: *Does this serve my intention right now?* If not, pivot.

**B. The 3 gates (for conversations):**
- *What am I amplifying with my attention here?*
- *What is my intention for this person (care, curiosity, celebration)?*
- *What's one small action that honors both?*

**C. Locus check (sovereignty cue):**
Whose agenda am I enacting—mine, theirs, or the algorithm's? If not mine, reclaim with a breath + re-aim.

---

## 4) Attention ↔ Intention Scripts (festival-ready)
- **Walk-in mantra:** *Where my attention goes, my energy flows; I aim it on purpose.*
- **Stage cue (eyes motif):** *"Look back at the looker."*
- **Consent cue (with friends):** *"What's your intention for tonight?"* → *"Mine is connection + wonder."*
- **Re-center line:** *"Pause. Breathe. Choose."*

---

## 5) Symbol: The Eye & The Arrow
- The **Eye** = selective openness (choosing what to let in).
- The **Arrow** = deliberate direction (choosing what to move toward).
- Practice: during a set, physically gesture *eye → arrow* to anchor state.

---

## 6) Failure modes & fixes
- **Hijacked attention** (lights, crowd, phone). → *Fix:* 1 breath + tuck phone; pick one sensory anchor (drum, bass, a friend's face).
- **Vague intention** (nice words, no aim). → *Fix:* name **one verb** (e.g., *listen, dance, care, rest*).
- **Over-intending** (rigid plan kills serendipity). → *Fix:* hold a **direction**, not a script; ask, *"What is this moment asking?"*

---

## 7) Daily field practice (Thu–Sun)
**Morning (2 min):**
- Write today's **North Star (one sentence)**: *"I intend to ___ so that ___."*
- Choose **3 attention anchors**: breath • rhythm • one friend • sky • feet on ground.

**Midday (30 sec):**
- ABC reset + quadrant check (from §2).

**Night (2 min):**
- 3 lines: **Noticed → Chose → Outcome.**
- One gratitude tied to attention ("I looked *here* and it gave me *this*").
